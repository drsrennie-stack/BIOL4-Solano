Spiral Rounds (study group method)

Spiral Rounds is your in-class and study-group method, organized by concept category. Each category has 1 or more sets of about 10 to 12 structures. Take each set through the five rounds below, escalating challenge each round.

Open standalone version

Group size: 3 to 5 students per Spiral Rounds set.

Pace: aim for 5 to 8 minutes per round per set. A set of 12 structures runs all 5 rounds in roughly 30 to 40 minutes.

Rule: every student participates. Active retrieval is the point. Mistakes are expected and useful. Don't opt out by watching.
