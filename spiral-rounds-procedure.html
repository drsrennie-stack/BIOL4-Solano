Spiral Rounds · BIO 004 Human Anatomy

The Five-Round Procedure

Keep this page next to your study group during lab. Run each set of 10 to 12 structures through all five rounds.

Ground rules

Group size: 3 to 5 students per Spiral Rounds set.

Pace: 5 to 8 minutes per round per set. A set of 12 structures runs all 5 rounds in 30 to 40 minutes.

Set size: 10 to 12 structures per set. Build from 5 to 8 in Round 1, add more as you go.

Participation: every student takes a turn in every round. Active retrieval is the point. Mistakes are expected and useful. Don't opt out by watching.
